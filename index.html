SPECIAL KERALA FESTIVAL TOURS

Kathakali

Kathakali is an evolved dance form popular in the South Indian state of Kerala. This Indian classical dance involves storytelling activities through fascinating footwork and expressive gestures of face and hands accompanied by music and vocal performance.

Thrissur Pooram

The Thrissur Pooram is an annual celebration celebrated on the pooram asterism in the Malayalam month of Medam in the huge Thekkinkadu Maidan in Thrissur city.

Aranmula Boat Race

One of the oldest traditions of Kerala, Aranmula Boat Race or Aranmula Uthrittathi Vallamkali is a grand event, held between August and September.
